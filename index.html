Meetings Schedule

PINE VALLEY SPECIAL SERVICE DISTRICT BOARD OF TRUSTEES MEETINGS

Meetings held first Wednesday of each month at the Pine Valley Fire Station, at 6:00 pm.

PVSSD meetings are open to the public. Please feel free to sit in on any meeting, giving your own thoughts on the subjects under discussion. Our goal is to keep Pine Valley a truly wonderful place to live. If you have concerns you’d like placed formally on the agenda, please follow this link to the PVSSD page for contact information.

PINE VALLEY HERITAGE CENTER

Heritage Center opens Memorial Day weekend and will remain open until Labor Day. Hours of operation are Monday through Saturday 10:00 to 6:00.
